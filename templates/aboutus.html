Finance Center

Do you struggle managing your finance at the end of every month? If yes, We have got a solution for you,Finance Center. Finance Center is a finance management application that can help you managing your financials anytime at your fingertips. Using This application , you will be able to:

  • Keep Track of your monthly expenses.
  • View analytics of your expenses and savings.
  • Manage all your Funds in one place.
  • Easily invest and diversify your portfolio in equity/options/futures, bank savings like FD/RD.

Technology STACK:

  • Python/django
  • django rest framework
  • PostgreSQL
  • HTML,CSS
